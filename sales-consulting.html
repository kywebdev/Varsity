A full range of sales-driving services

Because your challenges are unique to your community, we design a customized approach based on integrated, research-driven strategic thinking across brand, tactics and sales. Sales & Marketing services include:

  • Mystery shopping: Let us visit the competition to gain insights into their facilities and the skill level of their marketing staff.
  • Sales consulting and training: We help you to implement your marketing strategy, conduct training of both new and seasoned employees to enhance sales skills and ensure that your team functions at its highest level.
  • Competitive analysis: Our in-depth analysis will provide you with a full picture of your competitions’ brands, including competitive pricing, promotional programs, services, facilities, accommodations and sales skills.
  • Sales & marketing assessments: You can’t get where you want to go if you don’t know where you are. We’re skilled at performing in-depth audits, formulating analyses and recommending improvements.
  • CRM strategy and implementation: Customer relationship management (CRM) programs are at the very core of a community’s efforts. We can assist in CRM selection, implementation and ongoing oversight to ensure that data is accurate, and that effective follow-up strategies are in place.
  • Special event strategy and implementation: We can suggest and develop events that perfectly align with a community’s marketing strategies, provide speakers and marketing materials and manage all of the planning and execution details.
"It all begins with understanding your prospects."– Jackie Stone, VP Sales Consulting


Jackie Stone, VP Sales Consulting

Jackie brings with her more than 26 years of experience as a senior living marketing professional. She’s been on both sides of the table, working as a senior executive in sales, holding marketing and operations roles within Life Plan Communities, and working as a consultant to nearly 100 senior living communities of all shapes, sizes and levels of care across the country.

During her career, Jackie has been a partner in a senior living development company, where she led a team of professionals to implement the sales & marketing efforts of greenfield Life Plan Communities across the country. She has been a vital contributor in strategic and master campus planning initiatives; launched new home and community-based services that continue to be successful today; and developed senior living-specific sales training programs. She works closely with client sales & marketing teams to ensure optimal results from their marketing investment.

Jackie is a regular speaker at the LeadingAge Annual Meeting & EXPO, as well as various state LeadingAge conferences.
